OVERVIEW

Product Overview

🏆

Flagship Performance · Extreme Environments

The IG-S series uses solid ceramic non-inductive technology, featuring HXO's patented Ceramic Area Conduction Technology (Peeling Area Conduction Technology, CN 113016042 A), achieving industry-leading 40kV pulse withstand, 350°C extreme operating temperature, and <0.1μH parasitic inductance. Designed for high-performance racing, turbocharged vehicles, military/aerospace, and industrial extreme environments.

⚡

Key Specifications

  • Resistance range: 1kΩ ~ 20kΩ (most common: 5kΩ~10kΩ)
  • Power rating: 2W / 3W / 5W / 7W / 10W
  • Pulse withstand: 40kV (peak) — Industry leading
  • TCR: -900±300 ppm/°C (NTC negative temperature coefficient)
  • Parasitic inductance: <0.1 μH (non-inductive, DC~100MHz)
