OVERVIEW

Product Overview

🎯

Economy · High Vibration Resistance

The IG-F series uses fiberglass (GFRP) core wirewound technology, providing solid basic performance at approximately 30% lower cost than ceramic core series. The low elastic modulus and high elongation of fiberglass provide natural advantages in vibration environments, making it the preferred choice for aftermarket replacement and economy OEM applications.

⚡

Key Specifications

  • Resistance range: 1kΩ ~ 15kΩ (most common: 3.3kΩ/4.7kΩ/10kΩ)
  • Power rating: 1W / 3W / 5W / 8W / 10W
  • Pulse withstand: 25kV peak (1.2/50μs standard lightning impulse)
  • TCR: ±200 ~ ±500 ppm/°C
  • Vibration test: 2.0g, 3 hours/axis
