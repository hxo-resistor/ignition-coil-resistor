Replacing VISHAY/TE/KOA with HXO: Do You Need to Modify Your Circuit Board?

HXO Resistor · Import Substitution Guide · July 2026

"Will switching to a Chinese alternative require circuit board modifications?" This is one of the most common questions we receive from overseas buyers. Many customers are accustomed to imported brands and have concerns about compatibility — worried it won't fit, will require redesign, or add extra work.

Direct answer: HXO IG series replaces VISHAY/TE/KOA and other imported brands in the vast majority of cases without any circuit board modifications. Pin spacing, dimensions, resistance, and power ratings are fully compatible — direct drop-in replacement.

Why No Circuit Changes Are Needed

Ignition coil suppressor resistors are standardized components. Unlike IC chips with hundreds of package types, suppressor resistor dimensions, pin spacing, and nominal values are globally standardized:

  • Pin spacing: Standard 5mm/10mm/15mm — HXO covers all common spacings
  • Dimensions: Axial lead type, identical to imported brands
  • Nominal resistance: 4.7kΩ/5.1kΩ/10kΩ etc. — E-series standard values, globally used
  • Power ratings: 3W/5W/7W/10W — same ratings, direct replacement

✅ Verified by customers: HXO has completed substitution validation with 3 ignition coil manufacturers, with 100% pass rate. Customers soldered IG series directly into existing PCB holes — no circuit design changes needed, all test results passed.

Three-Step Replacement Process

  1. Provide your current model number — Tell us the imported brand and model you're using (e.g., VISHAY RTO series, TE CPF series, etc.)
  2. Get matched replacement — We recommend the corresponding IG series model, with pin spacing, resistance, power, and certification comparison
  3. Sample validation — Free 5-piece sample shipment. Test in your application, then proceed with bulk ordering
