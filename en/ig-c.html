OVERVIEW

Product Overview

🎯

Standard Workhorse · Best Value

The IG-C series is manufactured using high-purity alumina ceramic core (Al₂O₃≥96%) wirewound technology, providing the optimal balance of performance, reliability, and cost. It is the most versatile series for motorcycle and automotive ignition coil applications. With 30kV pulse withstand and -55°C~+275°C operating temperature range, it covers the vast majority of consumer ignition system requirements.

⚡

Key Specifications

  • Resistance range: 1kΩ ~ 20kΩ (most common: 4.7kΩ/5.1kΩ/10kΩ)
  • Power rating: 1W / 3W / 5W / 7W / 10W / 15W
  • Pulse withstand: 30kV peak (1.2/50μs standard lightning impulse)
  • TCR: ±100 ~ ±300 ppm/°C
  • High-temp load life: 275°C / 1000h / resistance change ≤ ±2%
